## FULL SCRIPT

**[0-4s] HOOK**
*[VISUAL: Me be em be, background am, text 'Thang dau sau sinh']*

"Thang dau cua be so sinh, me khong can lam hoan hao. Me chi can de y 4 nhom dau hieu nay."

**[4-16s] AN**
*[VISUAL: Binh sua/cho bu, tick checklist]*

"Mot la an. Be co bu deu khong, co tinh tao khi bu khong, so lan bu co giam bat thuong khong. Neu me thay be bo bu hoac li bi, dung cho qua."

**[16-28s] TA**
*[VISUAL: Thay ta, checklist uot/ban]*

"Hai la ta. So ta uot va phan moi ngay giup me biet be co nhan du sua khong. Mau phan thay doi trong thang dau la binh thuong, nhung co mau la hoac kem sot thi can hoi bac si."

**[28-40s] NGU VA THO**
*[VISUAL: Be ngu nam ngua, khong chan goi mem quanh mat]*

"Ba la ngu va tho. Be nen nam ngua khi ngu, cho ngu thong thoang. Neu me thay be tho rut lom nguc, tim tai, hoac ngung tho bat thuong thi di kham ngay."

**[40-52s] ME CUNG CAN DUOC CHAM**
*[VISUAL: Me uong nuoc, ngu mot chut khi be ngu]*

"Bon la suc khoe cua me. Me sau sinh ma met qua, buon keo dai, dau nhieu, sot hoac ra mau bat thuong cung can duoc cham soc, khong phai co gang chiu mot minh."

**[52-60s] CTA**
*[VISUAL: Man hinh bai viet tren dien thoai]*

"Mình de checklist thang dau cua be tren Me Biet Chua. Me luu lai de luc roi co cai xem nhanh nha."
